# Soul Story Card Rebalanced Specimen Design

## Goal

Keep the approved light, compact story-card composition while making its middle earn its space. The result should feel like a K-pop collectible, not a corporate dashboard or a sparse poster.

## Composition

- Preserve the existing top identity block exactly: 24px top margin, code, archetype name, English name, and one-line truth.
- Retain the light glass frequency panel and the subtle Silvercore grid. Do not use a dark navy panel or a strong coloured background wash.
- Grow the frequency panel just enough to contain a third, low-emphasis readout row: `入坑核心 · 個性 × 表演`.
- Place the readout inside the panel below the four frequency bars, separated by a quiet rule or spacing—not as a free-floating line in the card body.
- Keep the CTA and KSTAR date as a single compact footer block below the panel. Use a deliberate small gap between the panel and footer; do not pin the footer to the card edge.
- Balance the card by visual rhythm rather than forcing identical numerical gaps: the panel absorbs the explanatory middle content, while the header and footer remain clear bookends.

## Visual Direction

- Light chrome / white glass base, red signal accents, muted cobalt grid detail.
- Frequency panel is translucent white with a restrained cool-grey stroke.
- No dark dashboard panel, no heavy glow, and no additional decorative badges.
- Keep the existing KSTAR typography and corner stars.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The `入坑核心` readout is visually part of the frequency specimen.
- No large unassigned gap remains between the specimen and CTA.
- The card remains light, playful, and site-consistent at its native 270 x 480 size.
- The footer has comfortable but not oversized bottom breathing room.
